At Game Boy, we believe in making play portable. Since our debut in 1989, our mission has been simple: to bring joy, adventure, and imagination into the hands of millions worldwide. We pioneered handheld gaming with a device that combined durability, simplicity, and a library of unforgettable titles.

Our philosophy is rooted in accessibility — games that anyone can pick up and enjoy, whether you’re a seasoned player or just starting out. From puzzle challenges to epic quests, we’ve built experiences that connect friends, spark creativity, and inspire nostalgia across generations.
